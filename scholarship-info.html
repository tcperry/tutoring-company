A+ Scholarships

A+ Tutoring’s annual scholarship contest is open for its eighth year. Multiple prizes are available to apply for, including a first place scholarship of $10,000! A+ Tutoring encourages quality education with our tutoring services, but we strive to do more than that. We also help students advance to college, and we proudly offer scholarships to help students achieve their educational goals.

Apply today and begin your essay. Not sure how to start? Reach out to any of our tutors and get tips on how to put together a winning scholarship application!

Write

In 300 to 500 words, tell us: What have you learned from a hobby/activity, and how would you create a teaching lesson out of it?

Promote

Using email and social media, reach out to people to gain votes for your essay. Finalists are chosen by popular vote!

Make it to the final round

Tutors will evaluate essays in the final round to select the winners of scholarships worth $2,000, $5,000, and $10,000!

Deadlines

Essays must be submitted by May 7th, 2014. Winners are announced July 7th, 2014!
